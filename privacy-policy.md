# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 1, 2026**

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how this application ("Service") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our content scheduling tool.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- TikTok account authorization (OAuth token) to publish content on your behalf.

### Information We Do Not Collect
- We do not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- We do not collect or store TikTok user data beyond the OAuth token required to post content.
- We do not track browsing activity or use cookies.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

Your TikTok OAuth token is used solely to:
- Publish video content to your TikTok account as authorized by you.

We do not use your information for any other purpose.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- OAuth tokens are stored securely within the workflow automation platform (n8n).
- We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.
- Access to stored credentials is restricted to the application owner.

## 5. Third-Party Services

This Service integrates with:
- **TikTok** — for publishing content (subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy)
- **YouTube** — for discovering publicly available video content
- **Twitch** — for discovering publicly available video content

We only access publicly available data from these platforms.

## 6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as the Service is active. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings, which will immediately stop the Service from posting on your behalf.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time.
- Request deletion of any stored credentials by contacting the application owner.

## 8. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

## 10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the application owner.
